How it works

Four stages. Zero clicks per track.

01

Set your folder

Point DJTrackChecker at a folder with your downloads. No drag-and-drop per track.

02

Beatport identifies them

Each file matched against the catalogue. Artist, title, mix, BPM, key, genre, artwork: all correct and written.

03

Audio quality verified

Spectral analysis flags upscaled garbage before it pollutes your library. More checks coming soon.

04

Files renamed, filed

Clean matches land in your destination folder, following your personal naming convention. Uncertain ones go to the review queue.
