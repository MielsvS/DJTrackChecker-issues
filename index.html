How it works
1
Pick your folder, hit process — service mode on the roadmap
Point DJTrackChecker at a folder of freshly downloaded MP3s and let it run. No drag-and-drop per track, no manual lookups.
2
Tracks identified via Beatport
Each file is matched against the Beatport catalogue. Artist, title, mix version, BPM, key, genre, release date, and artwork are written automatically.
3
Audio quality verified
A spectral analysis flags files that claim to be 320kbps but aren't — upscaled garbage caught before it pollutes your library. More quality checks will be added later.
4
Files renamed and moved
Clean matches land directly in your desired folder, correctly named. Specify a subfolder format if you like. Uncertain matches go to a review queue — you stay in control.
